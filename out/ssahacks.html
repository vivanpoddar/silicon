FAQ

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a 24-hour event where students come together to create a project that solves a problem. Students can work individually or in teams of up to 4 people. At the end of the 24 hours, students will present their projects to a panel of judges. The best projects will win prizes!

Who can participate?

Any high school student can participate! You do not need to have any prior experience with coding or hackathons. We will be hosting workshops to help you get started on your project.
